I am a Staff Machine Learning Engineer at Coupang, working at the intersection of machine learning research and large-scale industrial applications. I obtained both my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from the Department of Statistics, Fudan University in 2012 and 2017, supervised by Prof. Ming Zheng and Prof. Wen Yu, working on semiparametric statistics and survival analysis.
